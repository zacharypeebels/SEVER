SEVER/ Privacy Policy

Effective: July 7, 2026 · Applies to the SEVER beta

SEVER is in beta. During the beta, bank connections use Plaid's sandbox environment only — no real bank credentials or live financial account data are collected.

Who we are

SEVER ("we") is an autonomous subscription guardian: it detects recurring charges and, at your direction, cancels, pauses, or renegotiates them. Contact: privacy@sever.app.

What we collect

  • Account data: your email address and a hashed password, managed by Amazon Cognito. We never see or store your password.
  • Subscription ledger: merchants, amounts, billing cadence, usage recency, and the actions you take (sever/pause/haggle).
  • Bank data (when live): transaction metadata via Plaid. We never receive your bank username or password — Plaid tokenizes the connection. You may connect multiple banks; each connection is separately authorized and separately revocable.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell your data. Ever.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers.
  • We do not store card numbers or bank credentials.

How your data is protected

  • Encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256 via AWS KMS).
  • Stored in an isolated database reachable only by our application servers — never exposed to the internet.
  • Your data is partitioned per account; no other user can access it.
  • All administrative access to our infrastructure is logged and auditable.

Who we share it with

Only infrastructure processors required to run the service: Amazon Web Services (hosting, identity), Plaid (bank connectivity), and GitHub (application delivery). Each processes data under their own compliance programs.

Your rights

  • Export: download everything we hold about you at any time — the "Export Data" button in the app.
  • Delete: permanently erase your account and all associated data — the "Delete Account" button in the app. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.
  • Disconnect: any linked bank can be disconnected individually without deleting your account.

Retention

We keep your data while your account is active. Deleted accounts are erased immediately from the live database; encrypted backups age out within 7 days.

Changes

We will update this policy as SEVER moves from beta to general availability (including when live bank connections launch) and note material changes in the app.

This document describes current practice for the SEVER beta and has not yet been reviewed by legal counsel.
